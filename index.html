About

Welcome to The Esthie Corner, your one-stop beauty haven where self-care meets comfort! I'm a licensed esthetician and hair care specialist with a passion for helping people look and feel their best. From smooth, glowing skin, to healthy beautiful hair, I offer wide range of services tailored to your unique needs - all in a relaxed, private, in-home setting.

At The Esthie Corner, I specialize in full-body waxing using hypoallergenic soft wax, perfect for all skin types, including those with sensitive skin or excessive facial hair. But that's not all, I also offer professional hair services including individuals and knotless braids, loc retwists, and starter locs, and a wide range of children hair styles. Whether you're looking to refresh your look or start your natural hair journey, I'm here to provide quality care with a personal touch.

My mission is to create a welcoming space where you can unwind, glow up, and leave feeling confident and cared for.
